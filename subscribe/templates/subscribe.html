Bid to Charge

Play a game to help research on electric vehicles

Electric Vehicles (EVs) are key to combatting climate change. However, our current electricity infrastructure will struggle to accommodate large numbers of simultaneously charging EVs.

One way of addressing this is to use auctions to distribute a limited supply of electricity to competing drivers.

To study this approach, we designed a game in which you take on the role of an EV delivery van driver. As you play the game, you will anonymously contribute to our research.
