What is the story behind EV Game?

You work for a delivery company. You drive an electric vehicle around town, aiming to maximize your profit by completing certain deliveries. Each delivery gives you a reward, but you also need to charge your car with the energy you estimate you might need for reaching the destination.

How do I see all tasks that might be available?

The "Possible Tasks" view shows all the possible tasks that might come up later. Each task has associated the probability of actually occuring as well as its reward.

How can I buy energy?

Each day, you can participate in an auction which gives you the chance to buy the energy you need. Winning is not a sure thing though, therefore you need to carefully place your bids and decide on the correct amounts and prices.

What happens after I finish the auction?

Once you leave the auction room, you will be able to see the tasks that have actually come up on that day. You will be presented with a number of task sets which can you perform, these being your optimal choices for the day. You might not be able to perform all tasks that are available, or even none at all, if you do not have the required energy to cover their total distance. Once you perform these tasks and get your reward, you will advance to the next day.

When does the game finish?

The game finishes after day {{ user.treatment.numberOfDays }}. If you want to keep playing, you can become an awesome EV Guru and you will be able to play the game for many days ahead!
